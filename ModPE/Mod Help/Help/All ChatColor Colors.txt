KEY CODES - The selector key codes are the same as the ChatColor Colors.

§0 - Black

§1 - Dark Blue

§2 - Dark Green

§3 - Dark Aqua

§4 - Dark Red

§5 - Dark Purple

§6 - Gold

§7 - Gray

§8 - Dark Gray

§9 - Blue

§a - Green

§b - Aqua

§c - Red

§d - Light Purple

§e - Yellow

§f - White

function modTick() {
